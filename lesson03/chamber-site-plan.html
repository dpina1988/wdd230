Overview

Purpose

The Duque de Caxias Chamber of Commerce is a hub for exploring valuable resources such as guides, workshops, and industry reports to support business growth and development and stay informed about economic trends. It also allows access to a comprehensive listing of local businesses, including contact information and descriptions, to facilitate networking and collaboration.

Scenarios

  1. What campaigns were conducted to help women-led businesses grow in the region in the last five years?
  2. What events aimed at small businesses will take place in 2024?
  3. What was the latest research carried out by the chamber in the region? Where can I get the results?
  4. How can I suggest new surveys to the chamber?

About Us

Welcome to Duque de Caxias, where business thrives and community thrives. Located in Grande-Rio, Duque de Caxias Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to enhancing the economic vitality and quality of life in our city.

Our chamber is a dynamic network of businesses, from innovative startups to established enterprises, all working together to promote growth and prosperity. Through advocacy, networking events, and community initiatives, we support our members in achieving their goals and making a positive impact.

Explore Caxias through our directory of local businesses, discover the rich history and vibrant culture of our area, and learn why Duque de Caxias is not just a place to visit, but a place to call home.

Join us in shaping the future of Duque de Caxias. Together, we build a stronger community.
